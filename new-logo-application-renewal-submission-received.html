Thank you for renewing your Austin Public Library Card!

To complete the process, visit any APL Branch location with your:

1. your valid/acceptable photo ID

2. proof of current residence


OR visit the APL Customer Service Dropbox and upload photos or scans of your photo ID and proof of residence.

After you upload your documents, we will process your submission within 2 to 3 business days. Once approved, you will receive an email regarding the status of your Library Card renewal.
